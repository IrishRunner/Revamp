Services

Bathrooms

Revamp's speciality is transforming dated and tired bathrooms into modern, elegant masterpieces. You'll be amazed at what can be achieved!

Extensions

Revamp does extensions as well as new builds and re-builds. For those with no space to go out the side or back of the house and with a suitable attic space, why not convert your attic with minimum cost for that extra room?

Decking

Construction using pressure treated softwood, hardwood or composite decking to meet clients needs. Revamp will construct a previously designed deck or design from scratch. We will incorporate any of your favourite features in to your deck such as lighting, benches, planter boxes, garden trellis, gazebos etc.

Plastering

Thinking of replacing those old stippled ceilings for flat skimmed ceilings? We cover all aspects of plastering. Renovational work a speciality.

Tiling

Ceramic, porcelain and natural stone tiles laid. When tiling your bathroom, we firstly remove all bathroom ware to ensure the neatest possible finish. For kitchen splashbacks, we remove all sockets and switches before tiling, and then refix over the tiles.

Electrical

Whatever your electrical requirements, we can get it done. Revamp is partnered with RB Tech-Elec (ECSSA registered) to cater for all your electrical needs. From adding an extra socket or attic light, to needing your house wired or rewired we can do it all. For new builds or total revamps, have you considered smart wiring to future proof your home?

Plumbing

Be it replacing your bathroom suite, adding an ensuite to your home, installing a shower or changing a washer on a leaking tap, Revamp can help!
